Services

We are one of the unique, excellent teams providing services for PhD research scholars from various universities and colleges globally. We specialize in technical content development, implementation, and proving outputs associated with recent research problems identified through detailed surveys on the same topic. We provide promising output without using any AI tools in content development.

We publish research articles in relevant, reputed journals according to the scope of the research work in:

  • • Scopus Indexed journals
  • • SCIE Indexed journals
  • • UGC Indexed journals

We have been in this field since 2010, with 137 success stories and 9 discontinued projects (due to time impact). Our team is young, with a strong technical background, and supported by an International English Trainer who motivates us to deliver robust, error-free content without grammar mistakes.

  • • Computer Science
  • • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • • Mechanical Engineering
  • • Physics
  • • Law
  • • Business Administration
  • • Tourism and Hotel Management

We develop our own algorithms and have already created a new algorithm named "Termite Flow Optimization" for worst-case scenario predictions. We are also working on the "Tiger Hunting Algorithm" for more accurate predictions.
